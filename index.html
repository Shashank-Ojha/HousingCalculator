Net Worth Over Time: Rent vs Buy

Mathematical Model

Renting Net Worth: Start with Initial Liquid Net Worth. Each month, add Monthly Savings (Renting). The entire balance compounds at the Expected Annual Market Return rate.

Buying Net Worth: Start with Initial Liquid Net Worth minus the Down Payment. Each month, add Monthly Savings (Buying) plus (Annual Tax Saved / 12). This liquid balance compounds at the market return rate. Total Net Worth equals this liquid balance plus your Home Equity (Principal Paid). Note: Home appreciation is not factored in here to provide a conservative estimate.
